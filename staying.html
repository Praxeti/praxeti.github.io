Places to stay during your visit.

Peterborough has many options for places to stay for all price ranges and will accomodate groups of nearly any size in terms of finding somewhere to sleep. These are some of the places closest to the city center to allow you to see as much as possible!

Black Horse

PE69PB. Located ~10 miles from the city centre, but even closer to attractions like Burghley House. Catered with a restuarant and bar.

Read more here.

The Pearl Hotel

PE12SH. Located in the city centre. Fully catered with adjacent restuarants and quick access to any facility the city centre offers.

Read more here.

Holiday Inn Express

PE26HE. Roughly 4 miles from the city centre. Catering options available, pets are allowed. Situated next to the east of england showground, making it ideal for events held there.

Read more here.

The Bull Hotel

PE11RB. Located in the city centre. Historical building dating to the 17th century. Fully catered and wined with easy access to everything in the centre.

Read more here.

Park Inn

PE11BA. Directly in the city centre - walking outside greets you with the high street. Fully catered, but restuarants are not even a short walk away.

Read more here.

Best Western Plus Orton Hall Hotel & Spa

PE27DN. Roughly 3 miles away from the city centre, but is in Orton Longeville - a nice place to see anyways. Swimming pool and wellness centre inside. Not catered.

Peterborough is a Cathedral City in Cambridgshire, England that has a population of just over 200,000. Whilst it may appear to be a very normal city, there are many landmarks and destinations that are worth checking out.
Peterborough boasts an impressive history with many heritage sites that are all worth checking out by anyone with a remote interest in history. But aswell as this, it has a strong arts presence in the form of several theatres and many places to see or go to eat!
This site will show you many of the best things to see and do, so have a look through the pages and see what takes your fancy!
